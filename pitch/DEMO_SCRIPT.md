# Q&A PREPARATION

## Likely Questions & Answers

**Q: Who else is using this?**
> "We've built this specifically for luxury island destinations. The Cayman Islands would be the flagship—which means custom development, first-mover advantage, and our full attention."

**Q: What about privacy?**
> "Fully GDPR-compliant and aligned with Cayman Data Protection Law. All interactions are consensual. Visitors choose to engage with the chatbot. No personal data is shared without consent."

**Q: How accurate is the wealth detection?**
> "85-97% accuracy depending on tier. Higher tiers are actually easier to detect because the signals are more distinctive. We can show validation data."

**Q: What if visitors game the system?**
> "The system looks at patterns, not single signals. You'd need to consistently fake a wealthy lifestyle across many dimensions. It's not worth the effort for any legitimate use case."

**Q: Can we customize the AI responses?**
> "Absolutely. The AI is trained on Cayman-specific data, local knowledge, and your brand voice. You control the personality and recommendations."

**Q: What happens to our data?**
> "Data sovereignty stays with the Cayman Islands government. We process and store data in compliance with your requirements. Full export and portability guaranteed."

**Q: How long to implement?**
> "6 months to full launch. We can have a beta with partner businesses in 4 months."

**Q: What's the ongoing commitment?**
> "$500K annually for hosting, support, updates. Includes 24/7 support and continuous AI improvements."
